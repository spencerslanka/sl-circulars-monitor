Implementation of the Drug Prevention Week in line with the  
“Ratama Ekata National Initiative” 
 
Your attention is drawn to the Public Administration Circular 29/2025 dated 26.11.2025 issued 
regarding the “Ratama Ekata National Initiative” for the eradication of illicit drugs. 
 
02. 
Taking into account the destructive impact of illicit drug use on the entire society, the 
Ratama Ekata National Operations Council has decided to declare the week commencing 01st of 
March 2026 as "Illicit Drug Prevention Week." marking the commencement of continuous 
drug prevention programs being implemented in line with the "Ratama Ekata National 
Initative". 
 
03. 
Attention should be focused on achieving the following objectives during the Drug 
Prevention Week. 
 
3.1  Controlling and suppressing the sale and distribution of illicit drugs 
 
3.2 Rescuing individuals addicted to illicit drugs from that destructive addiction and 
directing them toward rehabilitation 
 
04.  To achieve the objectives mentioned in item 03 above, it is expected that the following 
programs be planned and implemented within this week, with the contribution of officers from 
every ministry, department, institution, corporation, and statutory board. 
 
4.1  Awareness programs for staff, civil society, and school children. 
 
4.2 Publicity programs aimed at creating a broad social dialogue regarding illicit drug 
prevention 
 
4.3  Programs conducted with the active participation of officers and the community 
 
4.4 Various promotional programs involving buses, three-wheelers, and school 
transport vehicles
