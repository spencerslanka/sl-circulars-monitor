According to the Minute of the Management Service Officers' Service, published in Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 1840/34 dated 11.12.2013, officers of the Management Service Officers' 
Service must pass the Efficiency Bar Examination prescribed for Grade I within 05 years from the 
date of promotion to that Grade. 
 
02. 
Accordingly, please be notified that it has been informed by the letter of the Secretary of 
the Public Service Commission No. PSC/APP/4/62/2018 dated 03.03.2026 that the Commission 
has decided to extend the concessionary period, which was previously granted until 31.12.2023 by 
Combined Services Circular Letter No. 01/2024 for officers who have already completed 05 years 
of service in Grade I but lost salary increments due to the inability to pass the efficiency bar 
examination prescribed for Grade I, up to 31.12.2025. 
 
03. 
Furthermore, I kindly inform you to take the necessary actions to conduct the 
aforementioned training program, giving priority to officers who have completed 05 years of 
service in Grade I as of 31.12.2025.
